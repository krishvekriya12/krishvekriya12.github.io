03 About & journey

From first line to first million

I build mobile apps the way I'd want them built for me: fast, honest, and finished. No half-shipped features, no spammy permissions, no bloat.

Over the last three years I've taken eight apps to Google Play — GPS field tools used by surveyors and site engineers, productivity utilities, and finance apps. Together they've crossed 1.5 million downloads.

In 2026 I started Setubandh Tech — my own studio. "Setubandh" means building a bridge: between an idea and a product people actually keep on their phone.

If you need someone who can own the whole journey — architecture, UI, Play Store release, monetization — that's exactly what I do.
